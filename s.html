About the website

This is a website presents various public policies/Consultation being worked on by the government and allows citizens to share their feedback on them.Law makers will get reports of the feedback collected on the Consultation with detailed data analysis. These reports ensure that all of the citizens’ opinions on Consultation are taken into consideration by lawmakers. The website records responses/feedback from citizens and passes them to the relevant.  authorities
